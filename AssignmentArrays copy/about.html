About Us

Hawt Munchies is a proud Mexican female owned business established in 2020. We provide the freshest dulces enchiladas right from our hometown Dalton, GA and ship all over the US to MX.

If you love explosive, mouthwatering, extreme candy, then you NEED Hawt Munchies! We also carry polvitos, chamoy and love to do catering for all of your pangas!

What sets Hawt Munchies apart is that we love our GENTE and we want to show you we appreciate your support by offering you 10% off your first order. So, please subscribe to our newsletter and don't forget to check us out in all of our socials!
